Best Websites to Purchase

Ten online incense sellers in the United States that enthusiasts frequently discuss on Reddit and in other reputable sources.

  1. Shoyeido USA — official US store for Shoyeido Japanese incense.
  2. Nippon Kodo Store — official store for Nippon Kodo (since 1575).
  3. Japan Incense (San Francisco) — retailer specializing in Japanese incense.
  4. Incense Warehouse — large multi-brand US retailer.
  5. Scents of Earth — sticks, resins, woods, and ingredients.
  6. Kikoh Incense — US retailer focused on Japanese houses; helpful blog.
  7. Wild Berry — US maker selling direct (many fragrances).
  8. Gonesh — long-running US brand with direct sales.
  9. The Monastery Store — temple-style incenses (incl. Shoyeido).
  10. The Incense Dragon — retailer carrying classic Japanese lines.

Research notes

Example community discussions: see the r/Incense subreddit and posts where users mention buying Shoyeido and other brands online. Additional verification comes from each shop’s official site and community resources such as Olfactory Rescue Service.
